SSSDUDE-NUTZ… it all started with an equation. Doughnuts + Art = PERFECTION. Owner Bradley Taylor set out on a quest to find a good quality doughnut that reminded him of his favorite doughnut shop back home in Cali. Alas, it could not be done, so SSSDUDE-NUTZ was born. Bradley created a series of unique doughnuts and designed them to leave an everlasting impression.

Come in to see the delightful menu wall, the wonderful Bradley himself, or to devour the doughnut of the day. Chill for a sec or for a while. If you enjoy our company; we enjoy yours.
